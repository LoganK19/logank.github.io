Here are some articles with tips for staying secure online:

1. Kaspersky Lab - Top 10 Internet Safety Rules & What Not to Do Online
This article covers tons of subjects in relation to staying safe online, including browsing, privacy settings, downloads, shopping, and passwords. There is a lot of information here for general safety.

2. McAfee - 10 Tips To Stay Safe Online
This article covers similar topics to the first, though it also has information that was not covered in the first, such as firewalls and VPNs.

3. Mozilla - How to stay safe on the web
This article will help users that use Firefox make sure that they remain safe while using the browser. This information almost entirely applies to only Firefox users, but there are some general tips at the bottom of the page.
